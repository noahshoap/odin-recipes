Tacos

Description

An easy but flavorful dinner to throw together. Beef, tortillas, and your desired seasonings and toppings. It's a common weeknight favorite amongst Americans and it's clear why. With so many variations and its ease of preparation, it's a great choice for a weeknight dinner.

Whether you're looking to start a family Taco Tuesday tradition or just craving some tacos, keep on reading to discover how to make the best homemade tacos you've ever had!

Ingredients

  • 80/20 Ground Beef
  • Tortillas
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Desired toppings

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in pan. Drain grease when beef is cooked.
  2. Return beef to pan. Add desired taco seasoning.
  3. Allow beef to finish cooking in seasoning.
  4. Top tortillas with beef and desired toppings.
  5. Enjoy!
