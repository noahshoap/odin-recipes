Tacos

Description

An easy but flavorful dinner to throw together. Beef, tortillas, and your desired seasonings and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 80/20 Ground Beef
  • Tortillas
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Desired toppings

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in pan. Drain grease when beef is cooked.
  2. Return beef to pan. Add desired taco seasoning.
  3. Allow beef to finish cooking in seasoning.
  4. Top tortillas with beef and desired toppings.
  5. Enjoy!
