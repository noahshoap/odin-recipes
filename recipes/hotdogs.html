Hot Dogs

Description

An American cook-out favorite. Easy to make and rather simple, but can be topped with whatever you wish to create infinitely delicious flavor combinations.

Ingredients

  • Hot Dogs
  • Hot Dog Buns
  • Desired Toppings

Steps

  1. Cook hot dogs. Traditionally, grill them. But pan-frying will work fine.
  2. Warm up buns
  3. Place dog in bun.
  4. Top as desired.
  5. Enjoy!
