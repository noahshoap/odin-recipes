Smashburgers Recipe

Description

A smashburger is a style of cheeseburger. It is known for its thin, crispy patties that cook quickly. A favorite style of many and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 80/20 Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • High Quality American Cheese
  • Desired burger toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat a cast iron skillet or griddle to medium - high heat.
  2. Grab 2 to 3 ounces of your beef.
  3. Optionally, you can pat the patties out. Traditionally, you keep them in a ball and "smash" them on the cooking surface.
  4. The patties are extremely thin, so should be ready to flip after 60 to 90 seconds.
  5. Top with cheese
  6. Remove, place on bun, top as desired.
  7. Enjoy!
